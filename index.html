From San Jose

  • 6 in 1

    Combine six popular rain forest activities into a single day jam-packed with adventure on this tour to Braulio Carrillo National Park from San Jose. Fly down ziplines over the rain forest canopy, visit a terrarium and butterfly garden, walk along nature trails, and more, all with hassle-free round-trip transportation from your San Jose hotel and a typical Costa Rican lunch to keep you going.

  • Vip City Bus

    Vip City Bus has the best city tour in Costa Rica at your disposal. Our unique sightseeing double decker bus will take you to downtown San Jose from your hotel to witness the city’s main historical landmarks for an immersive and panoramic cultural experience and a better understanding of Costa Rica’s evolution.

  • Combo Tour

    Enjoy the 3 Costa Rican wonders, the best coffee tour, the splendid Poas volcano and visit the beauty of our characteristic flora and fauna.

From Jaco
